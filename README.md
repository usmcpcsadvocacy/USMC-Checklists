USMC PCS Checklists
Interactive, mobile-friendly checklists for USMC PCS moves. All checklists automatically save your progress to your browser.

Available Checklists
1. USMC CONUS > CONUS Accompanied Checklist: For Marines moving within the continental United States. Complete step-by-step guidance from receiving orders through arrival at your new duty station.
2. USMC CONUS > Guam Accompanied Checklist: For Marines relocating from CONUS to Guam. Includes specific guidance for OCONUS moves to Guam.
3. USMC CONUS > Hawaii Accompanied Checklist: For Marines relocating from CONUS to Hawaii. Includes specific guidance for OCONUS moves to Hawaii.
4. USMC CONUS > Japan Accompanied Checklist: For Marines relocating from CONUS to Japan. Includes specific guidance for OCONUS moves to Japan.
5. USMC Japan > CONUS Accompanied Checklist: For Marines relocating from Japan back to CONUS. Includes specific guidance for returning from Japan.
