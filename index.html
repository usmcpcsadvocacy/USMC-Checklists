How It Works
Your progress saves automatically. Each checklist stores your completed items directly in your browser — no account or login required. Progress is saved per device, so each family member can track their own checklist independently.
  • Click any checklist above to get started
  • Check off items as you complete them — they turn green automatically
  • Close the tab and come back anytime — your progress will still be there
  • Use the Reset button if you start a new PCS cycle
